About Our Story

In 2022, Arabella C. Chee looked to create a restaurant idea of her own in Rizal, Antipolo City. In the same year, she took a risk by founding their own burger restaurant and decided to name it Little Snack House. The owners dreamed of creating a burger restaurant in which the customers could not only eat, but one that offered a friendly and healthy environment. The restaurant's success led her to begin franchising with her concept, becoming operating restaurants.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To provide the highest quality product at the lowest possible price, while becoming a real part of every neighbourhood in which we open.

Vision

To serve high quality products and extraordinary dining experience without compromising the taste and reasonable pricing while working toward the greater good for our employees, community and environment.

Values

Little Snack House is dedicated to providing a quality workplace for all employees. This implies treating people with dignity and respect, demonstrating trust, maintaining an inclusive workplace that values diversity, growth and teamwork, ensuring a productive environment which is free from discrimination and harassment, and providing a safe environment.
